Vegetable Hakka Noodles - my indian version of spaghetti!!

Hakka is a life hack

When I say this is a life hack , I am not kidding you. with simple ingredients and amazing flavours,this dish is close to my heart and my kids LOVE it !!!!

Ingredients

Cooking time:25 mins
  • Oil-2 teaspoon
  • Vegetable (your choice)- 2 cups
  • Bell peppers-1/4 cup
  • Garlic-chopped-1/4 cup
  • Onion-chopped-1/4 cup
  • Plain vinegar-1 teaspoon
  • Boiled hakka noodles- Al dente
  • Soy sause-1 teaspoon
  • Salt and Sugar- 1/2 teaspoon each
  • Cilantro-chopped-5 stems

Steps

  1. Boil Hakka noodles al dente. This should take under 5 mins in roaring water.
  2. Heat oil in pan and add in the finely chopped garlic ,onion and saute for 2 minutes, Do not over saute garlic. It gives you a burnt taste
  3. Add in the chopped veggies and bell pepper. Saute on medium flame for 5 minutes
  4. Add in the boiled Hakka noodles and salt and sugar, saute for 2 minutes
  5. Add in the vinegar and sou sause and saute for a minute, dont saute more after adding vinegar
  6. Garnish with cilantro and serve HOT!!!
  7. By the way this is an excellent lunch box dish for kids!! Do try.
