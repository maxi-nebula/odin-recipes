Mushroom Qusedilla- my indian take on mexican delight!!

Roll it and Grab it

Are you travelling and want a quick bite that wont get your hands dirty?? You came to the right place and it takes less place !!!!

Ingredients

Cooking time:2o mins
  • Oil-2 teaspoon
  • Onion-chopped-1/4 cup
  • All purpose floor-1 teaspoon
  • Roti or Chapathis-3
  • Italian Seasoning-1 teaspoon
  • Salt - 1/2 teaspoon each
  • Pepper powder-1 teaspoon
  • Water-1/2 cup

Steps

  1. Heat oil in pan and add in the finely chopped onions and saute for 2 minutes
  2. Add in mushroom,pepper ,salt and italian seasoning
  3. Add in the water and let in simmer for 3 minutes
  4. Saute on low flame till the mixture is semi dry add in the all purpose floor
  5. Switch off the flame and let it cool off
  6. Take a chapathi and place 3 spoons of the mushroom mixture. Roll the chapathis and place a toothpick in the middle to avoid spilling out, the last thing you want is a empty roti
  7. Place it in a zip lock and have it on the go!!
