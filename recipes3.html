Vegetable Puloa- indians and rice are inseperable and here is a recipe to fall in love with rice again!!

Bowl and a spoon is all you need to keep going!!

On scale of 1 - 10 how hungry are you?? If you answer is anywhere above 5, you NEED this. this is our comfort food and food that we cherish and celebrate

Ingredients

Cooking time:30 mins
  • Oil-2 teaspoon
  • Onion-chopped-1/4 cup
  • Chopped veggies-1 cup
  • Coconut milk-1/2 cup
  • Salt - 1/2 teaspoon each
  • Ginger garlic paste-1 teaspoon
  • Water-1 cup
  • Basmati rice-1 cup

Steps

  1. Heat oil in pan and add in the finely chopped onions and saute for 2 minutes
  2. Add in the ginger garlic paste and saute till the raw smell goes away
  3. Add in the chopped veggies and saute for 5 mins
  4. Add in the salt ,coconut milk and water
  5. Let it come to a boil and add in the soaked basmati rice
  6. Close the pressure pan and bring the flame to medium, this is important or else you will end up with a bottom scroched pan
  7. Cook exactly for 2 whistles
  8. Let the pressure release naturally
  9. Serve hot with curd or raita!!
